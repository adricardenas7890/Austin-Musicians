About

Mohawk is a beloved destination bar and live music/event venue located on the corner of 10th and Red River in downtown Austin, Texas. Opened in 2006 as a place to showcase the best emerging creative talent from Austin and beyond, this venue is known for killer shows, good 'ol fashioned service, strong pours and excellent parties. Mohawk is a unique indoor/outdoor multilevel space in the heart of downtown, both built and driven by a team of Austinites who work every day to bring something unique to the table. Stop in for happy hour seven days a week and live music nearly every night. Private event bookings available. All Are Welcome.
